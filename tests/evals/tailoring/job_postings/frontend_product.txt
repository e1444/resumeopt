About the role

We are hiring a Frontend Engineer to help build and evolve our customer-facing web product. You will work closely with product managers and designers to ship polished, accessible user interfaces, and you will own the client-side architecture for a growing single-page application.

What you'll do
- Build responsive, accessible UI components in React and TypeScript.
- Collaborate with product and design to translate mockups into production-ready interfaces.
- Own the frontend build pipeline (Vite/webpack), including bundle size and performance budgets.
- Write component and end-to-end tests to keep the UI regression-free as the product grows.
- Instrument the product with analytics and work with product management to interpret usage data and prioritize the roadmap.
- Partner with backend engineers on API contracts, but you will not own backend services.

What we're looking for
- Strong experience with React, TypeScript, and modern CSS (Flexbox/Grid).
- Experience with component-driven development and a design system or component library.
- Familiarity with accessibility (WCAG) best practices.
- Experience with frontend build tooling (Vite, webpack, or similar) and performance optimization.
- Comfort working directly with product managers to define and iterate on user-facing features.

Nice to have
- Experience with state management libraries (Redux, Zustand, or similar).
- Familiarity with design tools (Figma) and translating design tokens into code.
- Experience running lightweight A/B tests or product experiments.
